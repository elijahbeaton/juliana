Juliana True Jones-Beaton
is an academic, writer, and doctoral student studying English at the University of Delaware.

She wrote her M.Arts thesis on gender, genre, and disability in Sylvia Plath's letters and journals. Her research primarily deals with the intersection of disability and gender in twentieth-century American literature and film.
